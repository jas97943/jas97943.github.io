Recruitment Locations

Nimbus Plaza

Located in the heart of beautiful Nimbus Station, Nimbus Plaza is the main hub of new Nexus Force members. From faction shops where you can purchase your gear, to hordes of fellow faction members to chat with, Nimbus Plaza has it all. Upon arrival, find Nexus Jay and talk with him to get started!

Nexus Tower

Towering above the barren rock of Crux Prime, the Headquarters of the Nexus Force is a testament to the power of Imagination. Home to the last shard of pure Imagination, Nexus Tower is both guard house and staging ground, and acts as the launching point for Nexus Force operations throughout the universe. Talk with Tower AI Nexus Naomi to get started!

Calling all Level 3 Agents! Crux Prime is under siege! The Maelstrom advances upon Nexus Tower alongside their new allies, the Skullkin! Gear up with the new Valiant Weapons designed by Doctor Overbuild and catch a Talon Dropship down to Sentinel Point Zeta to join the fight!
